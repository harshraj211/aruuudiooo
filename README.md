🌱 eKheti – Smart Crop Advisory System

eKheti is a smart web-based platform designed for small and marginal farmers to simplify farm management, reduce costs, and improve productivity. It integrates real-time market insights, expense tracking, crop disease detection, and advisory tools in one place.

🚀 Features

📊 Dashboard – Central hub for farmer activities.

📈 Market Prices – Real-time crop and fruit market price updates.

📰 Agri News – Latest agriculture and rural development news.

💰 Expense Tracker – Track farming expenses and analyze cost distribution.

🌿 Disease Detection – Upload crop images for AI-powered disease identification.

🤖 Chatbot – 24/7 interactive advisory assistant for farmer queries.

📅 Crop Calendar – Seasonal sowing, harvesting, and irrigation schedules.

🧮 Calculators – Fertilizer, seed, irrigation, and other agri calculators.

🔔 Notifications – Smart alerts for market trends, weather, and crop advisories.

🛠️ Tech Stack

Frontend: Next.js, React, TypeScript

Styling: Tailwind CSS



Hosting: Vercel

AI & APIs: Integration for crop disease detection & chatbot
